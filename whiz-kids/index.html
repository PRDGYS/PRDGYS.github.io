This is the landing page for the Whiz Kids NFT Collection.

A group of students from Yale, USC, The University of Texas, and Washington and Lee are working to make the metaverse and Web3 tech useful. Our first step? An NFT collection by young and talented people, for young and talented people.

We're moving fast, and are looking for people to join us.

Our core team has various strengths, but we all have some sort of technical background. We prefer technical people in every position. For artistic positions we don't care if you're technical or not.

Here are our open positions:

  • 2D Character Creation artists
  • 3D Asset Artists (Proficient in Blender)
  • Cybersecurity focused developer

If you're interesed in one of these positions, email seun.omonije@yale.edu with the subject line "Whiz Kids Interest", a resume, and a short description about your interests (1-3 sentences).

This page is under construction and will be changing frequently. We'll incrementally release information about our project in the coming weeks.
